FoneFlare helps you get the word out when you need assistance, or be "on-call" when you can help others. Even if Internet access is hard to come by after a natural disaster, or in a remote coverage area, use SMS to request help from responders in your zip code, or find out who could use your assistance.

Simple to use

No complicated connections or web sites. Just use your phone's text messaging capability to send up a "flare" when you need help.

Resilient

There's a good chance you won't be able to get a reliable data connection after a natural disaster strikes. FoneFlare uses SMS (text) messaging when there's no data connections, so you can still communicate.

Anonymous

Respondents won't ever see your actual phone number; everything is managed anonymously by the FoneFlare app.
